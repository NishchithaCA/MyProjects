Light Channels World Movement

Light Channels

    Light Channels are those human beings who voluntarily channel and spread the Light, bringing the Light from its subtler level to the human level.


    Light Channelling is bringing down the Light in the subtler level to the human level by intending strongly and then spreading it around the entire world.


    Light Channelling is spreading Love, Peace and Harmony carried by the Light.


    Light Channelling is spreading the Healing energies carried by the Light.


The Movement

    The Light Channels World Movement was launched by Guruji Krishnananda on 18th May 2008.


    The Movement is to Channel the Light daily for seven minutes when we wake up or when we go to bed from our own places.


    It is to spread Love, Peace and Harmony on this earth. It is to heal the earth wounded by our negative actions, thoughts and emotions.


    If many people Channel the Light, the impact will be more. If many Channel the Light at the same time, the impact will be much more.


    Anyone and everyone can Channel the Light. No preparation is required except the desire to change ourselves and the world.


    There are no conditions and restrictions. There is no membership. No Fee.


    This Movement is meant to empower every individual to change and create new destinies for the world.

The Technique

    Imagine the Light descend-ing and filling up your body.


    Imagine an ocean of Light above you.


    Then, experience the Love and Peace of the Light for a minute.


    Then, imagine the Light spreading around gradually to your home, locality, country and the whole world.



Channel Light everyday for 7 minutes in the morning and in the night. Heal yourself, Heal the World.

For more information visit : www.lightchannels.com
