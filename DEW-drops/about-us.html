Our Technology Partner

Life Link Eco Technologies Pvt Ltd

1

Founded in April 2001 by a group of people with an extensive background in Chemical & Environmental Engineering and Biotechnology.

2

Team had earlier set up and run the research arm with IIT-B and SBT is a product of this team effort.
