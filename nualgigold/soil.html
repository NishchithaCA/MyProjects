Foliar Spray Gold for All Kinds of Soil

Description

To be sprayed twice within 15 days before sowing the seeds/plants.

Benefits

1. Increases soil fertility.
2. Fast decomposition of leaves and farm yard manure.
3. After application, diatom algae develops in soil and increases oxygen in root zone. This leads to better hatching of cysts of microbes and earth worm present in soil.
4. Air circulation in the root zone will increase, which leads to better yield.
5. Soil will become soft and porous, increases water holding capacity.
6. It increases soluble micro nutrients in the soil.

Application

Add 1ml per litre of water and spray on soil.
