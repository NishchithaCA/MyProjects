Foliar Spray Gold for Plants and Crops

Description

Nualgi Foliar Spray Gold is a nanonutrient with required essential nutrients loaded on nanosilica.When it is sprayed on to Plants and Crops it absorbed through the stomata openings of the leaves and is lodged onto the chlorophyll. This package of nutrients boosts photosynthesis of all types of Plants and Crops. The plants and crops absorb more Carbon dioxide from the air and make a liquid carbon pathway and send it through the roots into the soil as oozuates.

Application

Add 1ml per litre of water once in 15 days (varies from crop to crop).
