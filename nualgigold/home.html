About Nualgi

Nualgi Gold NanoBiotech is an R&D and Manufacturing Company that delivers products based on nanotechnology. We use scientific measures to boost the photosynthesis process in our target areas. Our company was est. in 2005.

The founder of Nualgi is Mr. T Sampath Kumar, Chairman. He has developed this product as a valuable addition to agriculture, aquaculture, and water pollution control.

• Our product empowers nutrient growth in Plants and Crops.
• We provide solutions to increase Soil fertility with the addition of soluble micro-nutrients.
• The product is credible in implementing live food creation to assist Prawn and Fish culture.
• Our product also offers solutions for water bodies by removing bad odours, stopping mosquito larvae breeding, and clearing organic sludge.
